Jalen's TV Show Reviews

Jalen's 1st Pick: Jane The Virgin

Jane The Virgin follows a new mother, Jane Villanueva, who was accidentally artificially inseminated and had her whole life turned upside down. As a catholic, she was taught that sex is something sacred she should only given her husband upon marriage. We follow her as she balances her life between her true love and her baby's father. I give this shows a 9/10 because while the show is good, it's something you can only watch ones.

Jalen's 2nd Pick: Good Girls

Good Girls follows a trio of moms who find themselves in financial binds. They work together with the help of a gang leader, Rio, in order to carry out illegal tasks to make more money. These jobs range from robbing a grocery store, to kidnapping, to theft, to conterfeit. I give this show an 8/10 because the concept is rather unique and watching them try to balance their home lives with their illegal lives, is entertaining but some of the plot points are also a little generic and boring.
