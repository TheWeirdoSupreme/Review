Alyssa's Music Reviews

1st Pick: Take Care

Take Care is the second studio album by Canadian rapper Drake. It has a total of 19 songs, just has hits after hits. This album alone won 2 Grammy's: Best Rap Album and Rap Recording of the Year. I give this one 9.5/10 because it's long but also has so many songs that are just phenomenol, featuring a lot of good artists. My favorites from the album has to: Crew Love ft. The Weeknd, Cameras, and Practice.

2nd Pick: SweetSexySavage

SweetSexySavage was released in 2017 and also the debut studio album from my favorite R&B singer Kehlani. This album had me in a chokehold. The beats, the lyrics the rift of her voice was just amazing. She's truly a great artists. This one had a 10/10. Some of my favorites were: Peace of Mind, Escape, Distraction and Personal.
