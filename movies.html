1st Movie Pick: Shawshank Redemption

Shawshank Redemption is a movie about Andy Dufrense a man who is wrongly acussed for killing his wife after he found out his wife was cheating. He is sentenced for 18 year in prison He goes federal prision and meets his bestfriend and confidante Ellis Boyd Redding, that helps him understand the ins and out of prison to break out. Andy launders money from inside to prison to fud his life style outside of jail. Review: 9.5/10 because the dialogue between the characters are natural and flows. The cinematography is excellent, the director is able to capture to the complex and thrilling atomosphere of prison
